In the heart of the city, an unseen threat flickers to life. A series of mysterious fires are breaking out, and only you, a CP Ranger, can stop the devastation. "Fire Escape: Save the City" is an interactive 3D strategy game that puts you in the boots of a modern-day hero.

Your mission is to navigate a top-down view of the metropolis, guided only by a series of cryptic riddles delivered through unexpected channels. Each riddle points to a potential disaster. You must decipher the clues, identify the correct location on the map, and race against time to extinguish the flames. With each fire you put out, you'll piece together the story behind the chaos. Can you solve all the riddles and save the city before it's too late?

## ë£° How to Play

1.  Click the "Start Mission" button to begin.
2.  Read the riddle displayed at the bottom of the screen.
3.  Analyze the riddle and the city map to deduce the correct location.
4.  Click on the numbered hotspot on the map that you believe is the answer.
5.  If you are correct, you will extinguish the fire and receive the next riddle.
6.  If you are incorrect, a "False Alarm" message will appear, and you can try again.
7.  Successfully extinguish all five fires to save the city and complete your mission!
